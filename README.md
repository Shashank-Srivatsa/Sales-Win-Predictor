# 📊 Sales Win Predictor

## 🚀 What This Project Does

Sales teams don’t lose deals randomly — patterns exist.

This project helps sales teams **identify which deals are likely to close, which are at risk, and why**, so they can focus their time where it matters most.

Instead of relying purely on intuition, this system provides **data-backed visibility into deal outcomes**.

---

## 💼 The Problem

In most sales organizations:

* Too many deals are handled at once
* Sales reps don’t know which deals need urgent attention
* Forecasting is often inaccurate
* Time is spent equally on high-value and low-probability deals

👉 Result: **Lost revenue and inefficient effort**

---

## ✅ The Solution

The Sales Win Predictor enables teams to:

* 🎯 **Prioritize high-probability deals**
* ⚠️ **Identify deals at risk early**
* 📈 **Improve forecasting accuracy**
* ⏱️ **Focus effort on deals that matter most**

---

## 🔍 What Insights It Provides

For every deal, the system analyzes patterns such as:

* Deal size and discounting
* Sales stage progression
* Customer history and engagement
* Activity levels (calls, meetings, follow-ups)
* Sales rep workload and experience

👉 Output:

* Likelihood of closing
* Key factors influencing the deal
* Signals indicating risk or momentum

---

## 🧑‍💼 Who This Is For

* Sales Representatives → prioritize daily work
* Sales Managers → track pipeline health
* Revenue Teams → improve forecasting and planning

---

## 📦 What’s Inside

This project simulates a real-world sales environment with:

* Client companies and contacts
* Sales opportunities (deals)
* Deal activities (calls, meetings, emails)
* Pricing and line items
* Contract data

All data flows into a structured data system designed to reflect how real sales pipelines operate.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works (High-Level)

1. Raw sales data is generated and stored
2. Data is organized into structured tables
3. Deal-level features are derived from activity and history
4. Each deal is evaluated to determine its likelihood of closing

---

## 📈 Business Impact

With this system, teams can:

* Increase win rates by focusing on the right deals
* Reduce time wasted on low-probability opportunities
* Improve pipeline visibility
* Make more reliable revenue forecasts
